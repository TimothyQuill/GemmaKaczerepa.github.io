On a budget?

Before you go nuts generating pages of AI content, let’s talk.

I realise AI has its place; it’s fast, it’s easy and it’s entirely free. But when you let AI play content manager without a human lens, things get dicey.

You risk sharing stuff that’s off-brand, full of mistakes, overuses words like ‘delve’ and ‘tapestry’, and is just plain boring.

You wouldn’t want your audience to lose trust in your business or fall asleep halfway through your ‘About’ page.

This is where a human editor (me!) comes in.

I offer AI-assisted content that’s totally on-brand, factually correct, fun to read and ‘tapestry’-free – so you can stick to your budget and rest easy knowing what you’re putting out there is top-notch.

(Don’t worry – if you’re paying me to write copy from scratch, I’ll never cheat with AI and pass it off as my own!)
